About me.

My personal web page

Welcome to my website 👋 My name is Lucía and I am a Researcher in NLP. I currently work as a Machine Learning Software Engineer for the World Intellectual Property Organisation (WIPO – United Nations) at the ATAC center, for the development and deployment of Speech-to-Text solutions.

I have recently completed a joint Ph.D degree between University of Geneva (FTI/TIM) (specialization: Multilingual Information Processing) and University Grenoble-Alpes (LIG/GETALP) (specialization: Computer Science), graduating with the highest distinction.

I also hold a B.A. in Hispanic Philology from University of Navarre (Pamplona, Spain) and a M.Sc. in Natural Language Processing from Institut National des Langues et Civilisations Orientales (Paris, France).

Research interests: Speech Recognition – Automatic Simplification – Large Language Models.
